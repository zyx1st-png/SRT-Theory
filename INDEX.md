# Reading order

Start at the top; each step adds depth.

## Start here

1. **One-page overview** — the shape and force of the theory in a single page.
   - [`theory/SRT_one_page_EN.md`](theory/SRT_one_page_EN.md) (English) · [`theory/SRT_one_page_CN.md`](theory/SRT_one_page_CN.md) （中文）
2. **Quick start** — [`theory/SRT_quick_start.md`](theory/SRT_quick_start.md). A longer walk-through, with plain-language definitions of the core terms (`Ĝθ`, `d-value`, `Ψf`, `T_dir`) and a "what SRT is not" table.
3. **Core reversals** — [`theory/SRT_core_reversals.md`](theory/SRT_core_reversals.md). The five conceptual moves at the heart of the theory, each with the misreading it invites and the boundary that blocks it.

## Objections, discipline, and evidence

4. **Critical FAQ** — the hardest objections. [`theory/SRT_critical_FAQ_EN.md`](theory/SRT_critical_FAQ_EN.md) (English) · [`theory/SRT_critical_FAQ_CN.md`](theory/SRT_critical_FAQ_CN.md) （中文）
5. **Claim levels** — [`theory/SRT_claim_levels.md`](theory/SRT_claim_levels.md). How proven, testable, and speculative claims are kept separate.
6. **Testability** — [`theory/SRT_testability.md`](theory/SRT_testability.md). A prioritized sample of the experimental program, with falsification discipline.
7. **Open problems** — [`theory/SRT_open_problems.md`](theory/SRT_open_problems.md). A public ledger of what SRT has *not* yet closed, and what would count as progress.

## Technical / advanced

8. **Fisher geometry, energy landscapes, and the FEP** — [`theory/SRT_FEP_interface.md`](theory/SRT_FEP_interface.md). How they map onto `L0/L1/L2` — and why they are not bare identities.
9. **SRT and Predictive Processing** — [`theory/SRT_predictive_processing.md`](theory/SRT_predictive_processing.md). Concept-by-concept mapping to PP/FEP, plus the misreadings it corrects.

## Deep / source

10. **L0 metaphysics** — [`theory/SRT_L0_metaphysics_CN.md`](theory/SRT_L0_metaphysics_CN.md). The deepest layer: the founding propositions, in source form. Dense, and currently Chinese only. Everything above is, ultimately, a reading of this.

## Deeper material

This repo is a readable public layer. The framework also has a fuller formal core — axioms, equations, and canonical definitions — kept in the author's fuller materials. Curated summaries can be added here over time.

To extend it: add a readable file under `theory/`, list it above, and (if it helps an LLM) reference it in [`FOR_AI_CONTEXT.md`](FOR_AI_CONTEXT.md).
